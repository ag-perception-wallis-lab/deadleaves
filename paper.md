# Summary

- Generate dead leaves images with various leaf shapes (circles, ellipsoids, rectangles, regular polygons)
- Use GPU if available to speed up generation process
- supports sampling from different color spaces (RGB, HSV, Gray-scale) and directly from natural images
- Multiple options to change the area of the image covered by objects (sparse vs. dense sampling and position masks)
